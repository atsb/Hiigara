3D Import Notes:
Other formats.
Inform me about the other formats, necessary to you, and they will appear in the future versions.

The following is skipped in all formats:

  • The vertex and face normals - normals are calculated automatically
  • The vertex and face colors
  • Animation (except for Half-Life, Quake II, Direct X, XSI and 3D Studio).

Open GL and Hardware Acceleration
3D Exploration can work with 3D Acceleration graphics card that support hardware Open GL acceleration. For this purpose from the Render, API submenu select Open GL. The speed of 3D acceleration is 3 to 5 times faster compared with 3D Exploration on an identical 3D card without acceleration.

 

Why you need to register your copy of 3D Exploration ?
